abstract

  • UFO catcher is indubitably the symbol of Japanese arcade. Based on these machines, we are aiming to make a device with similar function using DNA origami that has the potential to capture and release tiny particles. The goal of this project is to create a DNA structure that can capture and release materials such as virus and protein, but not limited to these two.
  • The structure that this research aims to create is a structure consisting of an outer larger tube with an inner portion connected to a magnetic bead by streptavidin-biotin binding which serves as the part that stretches out to capture incoming particles. This extended portion will take shape of 2 loops which will be pulled inside the larger tube with the help of external magnetic forces, entrapping particles that entered the loops through Brownian motion.

PROJECT

    Idea, Goal

    Background

  • Assembly of nanomolecules is undoubtedly difficult, however since the development of DNA origami, a lot of new structures have arisen due to its nature. Based on Japanese game arcade icon: UFO catcher or commonly known as claw machines, we are making a structure that is analogous to it.
  • Using these DNA origami, we are aiming a structure that has the potential to capture small particles such as virus. By implementing the idea of animal control pole that is often used to capture feral animals, we are planning to devise a structure that is able to move and function in a similar fashion.
  • By using magnetic beads, the movement and the function of the structure is regulated through external magnetic field or magnets.
  • The goal of this project is to discover the potential of this structure and to prove that this is a feasible method to capture and even facilitate the transport of certain molecules.

Design

  • This structure will take form of an outer portion of tube and an inner portion of inner tube with two extended portion serving as loops to capture incoming particles. The inner portion is then connected to a magnetic bead (~300nm in diameter) using biotin-streptavidin attachment.
  • By using Cadnano, a structure consisting of an outer tube and two inner loops was achieved. The idea is that the inner tube can move up and down, and therefore able to catch incoming particles in the middle of the loops.

    • How are we going to make sure the inner tube enters the larger tube?

  • By having one long scaffold strand extending from the outer to inner tube, we were able to make sure that there are 4 points of attachment between the 2 tubes. It will be due to chance that the inner tube will fit in perfectly into the inside of the outer tube.

    • How are we going to make sure that the smaller tube can move if it is permanently bounded to the outer tube?

  • The connection between the middle tube and the outer tube is made to be 48 base pairs long. Therefore, the motion of the middle tube should be unhindered.
